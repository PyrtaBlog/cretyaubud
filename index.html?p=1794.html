REGULATIONS

  • JUMPING, SITTING, WALKING, OR STANDING ON THE EDGE OF THE POOL AND SWIMMING AT YOUR OWN RISK
  • Our pool area is available for adults 18 years old and above with minimum spending applied
  • Pool seating area reservation open until 11PM, reservation in advance is required
  • Arrival after 11AM first come first serve applies based on availability
  • We do our best to accommodate any seating request upon booking
  • Group booking requires a deposit payment and seating is assigned
  • Leaving your ID or deposit on arrival to secure your table in an area with minimum spend
  • Swimsuits, bikinis, and burqinis are permitted. Clothing (non-swimwear) and nudity are not permitted.
  • Pets are not permitted
  • For your safety, inflatable pool toys/floats are not permitted
  • No running or diving into the pool
  • No glass is permitted in or around the pool
  • Appropriate swimwear is essential & we request that you please shower before pool use
  • Please consider other guests and do not block the pool view for extended periods
  • Operational hours 08:00 am – 9.00 pm | last order 8.30 pm

SAFETY AND SECURITY

  • Please note that non-compliance with this rule will result in immediate removal from the premises without a refund. We take your safety seriously
  • We have zero tolerance for risk-taking behavior and our security team will confiscate cameras and delete any photos of dangerous activity.
